GETZIO DELIVERY

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Welcome to the Getzio Delivery Partner Network. This policy specifically addresses how we manage the data of our Delivery Partners to ensure efficient logistics and safe delivery operations.

Your privacy is paramount. This Privacy Policy details the data collection, usage, and security practices for the Getzio Delivery app, designed specifically for our delivery personnel.

1. Critical Information for Delivery Partners

To function effectively, the Getzio Delivery app requires specific permissions that are essential for the delivery process:

  • Background Location Tracking: This app collects location data to enable real-time tracking of your position by customers and our backend system, even when the app is closed or not in use, to ensure accurate delivery estimates and order assignment.
  • Camera & Media Access: We require access to your camera to allow you to take and upload "Delivery Proof" photos (e.g., photo of the package at the doorstep) to confirm successful delivery.
  • Phone Status & SMS: Used for secure authentication (OTP) and to facilitate quick calling of customers during a delivery via the app's interface.

2. Data We Collect

Beyond the critical permissions above, we collect:

  • Profile Information: Your full name, government ID details, vehicle information, and profile picture.
  • Earning & Transaction Data: Details of your completed deliveries, incentives earned, and wallet payout history.
  • Device & Logistics Info: Battery status, network connectivity, and device model to optimize the app's performance during long shifts.

3. How Your Data is Utilized

We use the data collected primarily to:

  • Assign nearby orders based on your real-time location.
  • Monitor delivery progress and ensure timely service.
  • Verify delivery completion through proof-of-delivery photos.
  • Calculate and process your weekly or daily earnings accurately.
  • Enable customers to track their order while you are in transit.

4. Information Sharing

Your data is shared only with parties essential to the delivery ecosystem:

  • Customers: Receive your name, photo, and real-time location only while you are actively delivering their specific order.
  • Merchant Partners: Receive your arrival status to prepare orders for pickup.
  • Security/Compliance: We may share data with legal authorities if required for safety or fraud investigation.

5. Your Rights and Choices

As a Getzio Delivery Partner, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, including your earning history and profile details.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information in your profile, such as vehicle details or contact info.
  • Deletion: Request the complete deletion of your account and associated data. This will terminate your partner status.
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from non-essential marketing communications or promotional notifications.
  • Location Control: While background location is required for active deliveries, you can disable location services in your device settings or log out of the app when not on shift.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at getzio.official@gmail.com or navigate to the profile settings within the Getzio Delivery app.

6. Data Security & Retention

We employ enterprise-grade encryption for all data. Location data history is periodically archived and is only accessible to authorized personnel for quality control and dispute resolution. You may request account deletion through the partner portal at any time.
